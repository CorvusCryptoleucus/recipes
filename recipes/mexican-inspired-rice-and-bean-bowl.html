Mexican-Inspired Rice and Bean Bowl

A flavorful, filling dish using simple ingredients and pantry staples.

Ingredients

  • 195 g (1 cup) cooked black beans (or canned, drained and rinsed)
  • 187.5 g (3/4 cup) super sweet whole kernel corn
  • 200 g (1 cup) diced tomatoes, drained
  • 180 g (1 cup) long-grain white rice
  • 480 ml (2 cups) vegetable broth
  • 2.1 g (1 tsp) ground cumin
  • 2.5 g (1 tsp) chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional toppings: shredded plant-based mozzarella, salsa, or sliced olives

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice:
  2. Rinse the rice thoroughly under cold water.

  3. In a medium saucepan, combine the rice and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for about 15–18 minutes, or until the liquid is absorbed and the rice is tender. Remove from heat and let it rest, covered, for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork.

  4. Prepare the bean and vegetable mixture:

  5. In a large skillet over medium heat, combine the black beans, corn, diced tomatoes, cumin, and chili powder. Stir well to combine.

  6. Cook for about 5–7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until heated through.

  7. Assemble the bowl:

  8. Divide the cooked rice into serving bowls.
  9. Spoon the bean and vegetable mixture over the rice.

  10. Add salt to taste.

  11. Optional: Top with plant-based mozzarella, salsa, or sliced olives for added flavor.
